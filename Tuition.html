Tutition

Any level, any age, any ability. Learn Russian and widen your horizons!

Students: Russian is recognised as a challenging and relevant subject, and we have a 100% A*- B pass rate across GCSE and A-level, meaning you will be studying with the very best teachers and can expect to gain a valuable addition to your qualification portfolio.

Children: our child friendly lessons are fun and highly interactive; indeed the best time to learn is early when learning a language can develop transferable skills, giving children a head start in other subjects.

Adults: Our one-to-one classes give 100% contact time with the teacher, allowing you to improve your understanding much more effectively by providing a structured and personalised session to suit you. Alternatively learning in a group is a fun and social way to get plenty of speaking practice whilst still having an expert on hand to clarify and explain any questions you may have.

Learn topics that are modern, useable and relevant, for example how to communicate in practical situations such as in the airport or in a restaurant, or learn to communicate professionally with your Russian colleagues with our corporate courses.
